So I saw Marcus today — he came in complaining of a sore throat that started yesterday. He says it hurts to swallow, hurts to talk, basically just hurts in general. No cough, no runny nose. He did have a fever last night, 101 and some change, and he took some Tylenol for it.

On exam, his tonsils were red and swollen with white patches, and he had tender submandibular lymphadenopathy. Went ahead and did a rapid strep test, which came back positive.

So I'm prescribing amoxicillin, 500 milligrams three times a day for ten days. I counseled him to finish the full course even when he starts feeling better. Told him he should feel noticeably better within 24 to 48 hours. For symptom management, I recommended continuing Tylenol or ibuprofen for the pain and fever, and suggested warm tea or cold things like popsicles for throat comfort.

I also let him know he's contagious until he's been on the antibiotic for at least 24 hours, so I told him to stay home from work tomorrow and he should be okay to return the day after, as long as he's feeling up to it.

Return precautions given — I told him to come back in if the fever doesn't break within 48 hours of starting the antibiotic, or if he develops any difficulty breathing or trouble swallowing liquids.

No known allergy to amoxicillin or penicillin. Prescription sent to his pharmacy.
